WORK IN PROGRESS
BeamSkin Studio is usable but still in active development — crashes are rare, but bugs are possible.

Known limitations:
• Game updates may rename textures, breaking some skins until patched.
• Vehicle variants (box trucks, ambulances, etc.) are not supported.
• Mod support is present but limited — not all mods work.
• Some features are still missing or incomplete.
• Linux version is completely untested — may not work.
